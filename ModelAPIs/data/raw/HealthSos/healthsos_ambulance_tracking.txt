Ambulance tracking is an essential component of the HealthSOS emergency response system.

The platform tracks the availability and location of ambulances in real time. Ambulance providers can update their status through an integrated dashboard.

The system performs the following tasks:

Identifies the nearest available ambulance

Sends dispatch notifications

Tracks ambulance movement

Updates estimated arrival time

Users and their emergency contacts can view the live location of the ambulance responding to their request.

This transparency reduces anxiety during emergencies and ensures efficient coordination between patients and responders.
