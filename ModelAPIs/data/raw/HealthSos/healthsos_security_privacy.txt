Security and privacy are fundamental principles of the HealthSOS platform.

The system stores sensitive user information including medical records and location data. Therefore, strong security measures are implemented.

These include:

End-to-end encryption for emergency communications

Secure authentication mechanisms

Role-based access control

Data anonymization for analytics

Secure storage of medical information

Only authorized medical responders are allowed to access user medical profiles during emergencies.

All data access events are logged for auditing and compliance purposes.
