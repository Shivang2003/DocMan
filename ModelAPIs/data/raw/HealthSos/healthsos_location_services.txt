Location services are responsible for accurately identifying and tracking the user's position during emergencies.

The system uses GPS data obtained from the user's mobile device. In cases where GPS signals are weak, the system may also use:

Wi-Fi positioning

Cellular tower triangulation

The location service continuously updates the user's position and shares it with emergency responders.

The service also calculates optimal routes for ambulances to reach the patient quickly.

High location accuracy is essential for minimizing response time and ensuring successful emergency intervention.
