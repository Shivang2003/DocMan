The HealthSOS platform can be extended with several advanced features to improve emergency response capabilities.

Possible future enhancements include:

AI-Based Emergency Detection
Smartphone sensors such as accelerometers and gyroscopes can detect accidents automatically and trigger emergency alerts.

Wearable Device Integration
Smartwatches and fitness trackers can detect abnormal heart rates or falls and activate emergency assistance.

AI Triage System
Artificial intelligence can analyze symptoms and determine the severity of a medical situation.

Drone-Based Emergency Delivery
Medical drones could deliver first aid kits or life-saving medications to remote locations.

Predictive Emergency Response
Machine learning models can predict high-risk areas and pre-position ambulances accordingly.

These enhancements would further strengthen the HealthSOS ecosystem.
