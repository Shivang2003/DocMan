HealthSOS integrates with hospital databases to provide real-time information about nearby healthcare facilities.

The hospital integration module stores data such as:

Hospital name

Location coordinates

Available departments

Emergency room capacity

Ambulance availability

Contact information

When an emergency occurs, the system calculates the distance between the user and nearby hospitals using geolocation algorithms.

Hospitals are ranked based on proximity and availability of emergency services.

The platform may also send automated alerts to hospitals informing them of incoming emergency patients.

This integration improves coordination between emergency responders and healthcare providers.
